FAQ

Common Questions

What control panels does SMEC manufacture?

SMEC manufactures PLC panels, MCC panels, PCC panels, SCADA panels, RTU panels, VFD panels, marine control panels, ESD panels and custom industrial automation systems.

Does SMEC provide turnkey automation projects?

Yes. SMEC provides engineering, manufacturing, programming, testing, installation, commissioning and long-term technical support under one roof.

Can SMEC upgrade obsolete PLC and SCADA systems?

Yes. SMEC specializes in legacy system migration, modernization and retrofit projects across industrial sectors including Siemens S5 or S7, Allen Bradley SLC500 and obsolete marine PMS platforms.

Which industries does SMEC serve?

SMEC serves Oil & Gas, Marine, Offshore, Power Generation, Manufacturing, Utilities, EPC and Heavy Industries.

Does SMEC support offshore drilling rigs and vessels?

Yes. SMEC supports offshore rigs, production facilities, vessels, shipyards and marine operators globally.

Can SMEC manufacture custom control panels?

Yes. SMEC designs and manufactures custom PLC, MCC, PCC, LT, RTU, SCADA and electrical control panels based on client specifications, site conditions and industry requirements.

Does SMEC provide installation and commissioning services?

Yes. SMEC supports FAT, SAT, installation, commissioning, troubleshooting, maintenance and training.

How quickly can I receive a quotation?

Most enquiries receive an initial technical review within 24 hours of submission.

Can SMEC retrofit or upgrade existing control panels?

Yes. SMEC supports control panel retrofit, MCC refurbishment, PLC migration, obsolete component replacement, wiring upgrades and switchboard modernization.

Do you manufacture oil and gas control panels?

Yes. SMEC manufactures control panels for offshore, onshore, refinery, petrochemical and hazardous area applications including ESD, F&G, RTU and process control panels.

Do you support marine control panels and switchboards?

Yes. SMEC supports marine switchboards, emergency switchboards, PMS panels, vessel automation panels and marine electrical retrofit projects.

Which regions does SMEC support?

SMEC supports projects across India, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman and selected global project locations.
